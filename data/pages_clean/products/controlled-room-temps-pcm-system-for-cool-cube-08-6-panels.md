# Controlled Room Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 08

**Summary:** A set of six panels containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. These panels for the Cool Cube™ 08 provide advanced thermal protection for room temp product due to a 21.5°C/70.7°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Controlled Room Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 08 (6 Panels) (CC-PCMS-R08) is used to create a 15° to 25°C or 20° to 24°C environment within Cool Cube™ 08 models. This phase change material system of panels can be utilized to transition any Cool Cube™ 08 into one to manage room temperature product – ideal for FFPE, platelets and biospecimens.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Stacked (storage):** 12¼” x 7¾” x 6”
**Assembled Inside Pack-Out Space:** 11¼” x 6½” x 6½”
**Weight:** 9½ lbs.
