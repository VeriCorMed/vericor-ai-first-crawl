# Functional Needs Bed w/ IV Pole & Rails

**Summary:** A medical surge bed for the general population. This 18” tall (wheelchair transferable height) bed with IV pole & side rails offers safety/comfort to patients. It is intended for multiple initiatives over extended periods of time.

The Functional Needs Bed (RB-FN400) is a medical surge bed for patients with functional needs. This shorter, wheelchair transferable height bed allows easier in and out for all patients. In the event of an overflow, it doubles effectively as a temporary cot. It is lightweight and portable, has a five-position head elevator, two-position foot elevator, anti-microbial mattress, spring and deck design, a patient restraint belt, and heavy-duty locking legs. With a 450 pound working capacity, the sleeping surface is 32″ x 80″ at a height of 18″. This bed meets FED STD 1633 for flammability and FEMA guidelines, and is ADA compliant.

### Technology & Features

-

2” convoluted foam mattress on top of a spring and deck design.

-

Heavy-duty leg locks on all legs.

-

Breaks down and stores away in a compact footprint.

### Additional Features

* Two position Trendelenburg foot elevator.
* Five position head elevator with locking cord.

* Antimicrobial soil resistant mattress.
* Stow on board IV pole and side rails included.

### Dimensions & Specs

**In Use:** 80” x 32” x 18”
**Folded (storage):** 42” x 34” x 8”
**Weight:** 38 lbs.
