The Immediate Treatment with Special Procedures Module Hospital Triage & Treatment Module provides the components necessary for medical staff to care for casualties who require lifesaving care within a short time. This piece of the complete Triage & Treatment System is utilized at the scene of an accident or mass-casualty incident. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly. It includes 2 Rolling Chests, 1 Workstation w/ 6 Drawers & Table, 1 Wheeled Medical Case, 8 Upright Medical Organizers, and supplies/equipment to address the following care functions: staff personal protective equipment, examination, intravenous therapy, orthopedics, wound care, airway management, respiratory therapy, GI/suction, and wound closure.
