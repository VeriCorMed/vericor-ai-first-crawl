# Temp-Shield™ Insulation Lid of Cool Cube™ 03

**Summary:** A vacuum insulation panel that acts as the lid of the Cool Cube™ 03. This panel provides advanced thermal protection for temperature sensitive product by utilizing vacuum insulation technology for the lid of the cooler.

The Temp-Shield™ Insulation Lid of Cool Cube™ 03 (CC-VIPLID-03) is a replacement lid for the original insulation system of the Cool Cube™ 03. The lid panel is a rigid board of powder, shrink wrapped, encased with an aluminum membrane skin, and sealed under vacuum.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Lid only. Tape included for attaching.** 
**Outside:** 10” x 10” x 1”
**Weight:** 1 lb.
