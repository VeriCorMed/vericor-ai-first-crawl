# Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 96

**Summary:** An outer case that snugly fits the Temp-Shield™ Insulation System to create the portable Cool Cube™ 96 PCM cooler. This robust case is a standard tan with wheels, a tow strap, external pocket, and lifting handles. *(VIP & PCM Panels not included.)*

The Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 96 (CC-Case-96) is a hard-sided, heavy-duty case customized to fit the Temp-Shield™ vacuum insulated panel (VIP) system of Cool Cube™ 96 models. The case features a folding handle on top for pulling the unit, fold-down side handles for easy, two-person lifting, double-throw latches for easy opening, and a Tru-Trac System™ of wheels for ease of movement. This case is specifically designed to facilitate easy product transport.

### Technology & Features

 **![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-1-150x150.png)Lifting Handle**

Two sturdy fold-down handles (one on each side) flip up when lifting is required.

**![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-2-150x150.png)External Pocket**

An elastic, external pocket holds temperature monitors of various sizes and shapes. A hole with a flexible grommet allows the pass-through of a probe cord.

**![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-3-150x150.png)Tru-Trac System™**

Four multi-directional wheels enable maneuvering in tight spaces. Two locking casters secure the load when desired. Wheels are removable if not needed.

### Additional Features

- Padlock protectors.
- Six easy-open, double-throw latches.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Outside:** 26½" x 26½" x 28¼
**Weight:** 35 lbs.
