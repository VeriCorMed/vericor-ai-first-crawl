# Fridge Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 96 (6 Panels)

**Summary:** A set of six panels containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. These panels for the Cool Cube™ 96 provide advanced thermal protection for refrigerated product due to a 4.5°C/40.1°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Fridge Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 96 (6 Panels) (CC-PCMS-BV96) is used to create a 2° to 8°C, 1° to 10°C, or 1° to 6°C environment within Cool Cube™ 96 models. This phase change material system of panels can be utilized to transition any Cool Cube™ 96 into one to manage refrigerator temperatures – ideal for fresh vaccine, medicine and blood products.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Stacked (storage):** 19¼” x 19¼” x 6”
**Assembled Inside Pack-Out Space:** 18” x 18” x 18”
**Weight:** 47 lbs.
