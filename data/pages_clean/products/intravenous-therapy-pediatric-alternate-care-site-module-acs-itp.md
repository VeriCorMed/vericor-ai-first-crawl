The Intravenous Therapy Pediatric Alternate Care Site Module (ACS-ITP) includes the components necessary to administer fluid hydration and antibiotics to pediatric patients. This ACS (Alternate Care Site) module is utilized during a patient surge in the wake of a disaster. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly™. It includes 1 Upright Medical Organizer (UMO) within a Primary Medical Case, Pressure Infuser Bags, Intraosseous Needles, IV Extension Sets, Start Kits, Fluids, and more.
