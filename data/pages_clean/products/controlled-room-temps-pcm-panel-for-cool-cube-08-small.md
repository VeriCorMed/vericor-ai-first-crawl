# Controlled Room Temps PCM Panel for Cool Cube™ 08, Small

**Summary:** A panel containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. This single, smaller panel for the Cool Cube™ 08 provides advanced thermal protection for room temp product due to a 21.5°C/70.7°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Controlled Room Temps PCM Panel for Cool Cube™ 08, Small (CC-PCMP-R08-S) is a reusable panel used to create a 15° to 25°C or 20° to 24°C environment within Cool Cube™ 08 models. This phase change material panel is used to manage room temperature product – ideal for FFPE, platelets and biospecimens.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Outside:** 7¾” x 7¾” x 1”
**Inside Edge:** 6½” x 6½”
**Weight:** 1 lb.
