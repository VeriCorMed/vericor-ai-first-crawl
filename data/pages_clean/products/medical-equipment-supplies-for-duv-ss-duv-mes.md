# Medical Equipment & Supplies (for DUV)

**Summary:** A medical kit used to supplement Dual Use Vehicles (DUVs) with equipment and supplies necessary to evacuate ambulatory, wheel-chair and litter patients during emergencies or disasters. (Made specifically for the Dept. of Veterans Affairs.)

The Medical Equipment & Supplies for DUV (SS-DUV-MES) is a module of equipment and supplies for the evacuation of ambulatory, wheel-chair and litter patients during emergencies or disasters. This basic life support (BLS) level of care module is intuitively organized, compactly stored, rapidly deployed, and easily maintained. The included SmartBook™ contains photos and critical data of all equipment and supplies (digital and print).

### Technology & Features

-

Provides the supplementary equipment and supplies necessary to evacuate patients during emergencies or disasters.

-

Module come ready to store and is can be deployed on a moments notice.

-

SmartBook™ system guide simplifies inspection, streamlines replenishment, and aides in deployment and training.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Palletized:** 48” x 40” x 72”
**Weight:** 320 lbs.
