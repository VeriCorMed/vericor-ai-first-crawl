# Rolling Rack for 8 Response Beds

**Summary:** A rolling storage and deployment rack for 8 Functional Needs Beds or 12 General-Purpose Beds. This heavy duty steel rack (at trailer height) has 4 shelves along with fixed and swivel casters for easy movement on most surfaces.

The Rolling Rack for 8 Response Beds (RR-8) is a rolling storage and deployment rack for 8 Functional Needs Beds or 12 General-Purpose Beds. This rack is constructed of heavy-duty steel with a baked-on, powder-coat finish to protect it from dings and scratches during exercises and/or deployment. Four wheels (two swivel and two fixed) ensure dynamic maneuverability and ease of movement on almost any surface. The rack features a welded-steel construction with laser-cut components for no sharp edges, and fits in typical trailers. Let these four shelves support the beds needed for those emergency preparation and mitigation plans.

### Technology & Features

-

Welded steel construction with laser cut components with no sharp edges.

-

Baked-on, powder-coating finish.

-

Two fixed and two swivel, 3" diameter wheels.

**H x D x W:** 68” x 43” x 38”
**Weight:** 140 lbs.
