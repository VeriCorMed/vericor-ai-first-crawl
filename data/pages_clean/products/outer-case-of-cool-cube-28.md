# Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 28

**Summary:** An outer case that snugly fits the Temp-Shield™ Insulation System to create the portable Cool Cube™ 28 PCM cooler. This robust case is a standard tan with wheels, a tow handle, external pocket, and lifting handles. *(VIP & PCM Panels not included.)*

The Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 28 (CC-Case-28) is a hard-sided, heavy-duty case customized to fit the Temp-Shield™ vacuum insulated panel (VIP) system of Cool Cube™ 28 models. The case features a clip-on towing strap for pulling the unit, fold-down side handles for easy, two-person lifting, double-throw latches for easy opening, and four swivel wheels (two locking) for ease of movement. This case is specifically designed to facilitate easy product transport.

### Technology & Features

 **![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-1-150x150.png)Towing Handle**

An integrated towing handle is ready to pull with an easy toggle release. Then, two sturdy fold-down side handles flip up when lifting is required.

**![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-2-150x150.png)External Pocket**

An elastic, external pocket holds temperature monitors of various sizes and shapes. A hole with a flexible grommet allows the pass-through of a probe cord.

**![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-3-150x150.png)Tru-Trac System™**

Swivel, front wheels and rigid rear wheels (all removable) track effortlessly behind when being pulled. Wheels are removable if not needed.

### Additional Features

- Padlock protectors.
- Six easy-open, double-throw latches.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Outside:** 20½" x 20½" x 23"
**Weight:** 22 lbs.
