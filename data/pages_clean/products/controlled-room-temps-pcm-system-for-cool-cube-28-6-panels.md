# Controlled Room Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 28

**Summary:** A set of six panels containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. These panels for the Cool Cube™ 28 provide advanced thermal protection for room temp product due to a 21.5°C/70.7°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Controlled Room Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 28 (6 Panels) (CC-PCMS-R28) is used to create a 15° to 25°C or 20° to 24°C environment within Cool Cube™ 28 models. This phase change material system of panels can be utilized to transition any Cool Cube™ 28 into one to manage room temperature product – ideal for FFPE, platelets and biospecimens.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Stacked (storage):** 13¼” x 13¼” x 6”
**Assembled Inside Pack-Out Space:** 12” x 12” x 12”
**Weight:** 21 lbs.
