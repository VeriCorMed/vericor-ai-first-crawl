# 3-Drawer Mobile Medical Case

**Summary:** A durable 3-drawer case built to keep lifesaving equipment and supplies organized, secure and clean. This case converts from a storage container to a medical cabinet in seconds and is airtight making it ideal for emergency preparedness supplies.

The 3-Drawer Mobile Medical Case (MC-3D) is a storage container, cabinet and mobile case all-in-one. This durable case provides deep widths and easy access to vital medical gear in the field by taking only seconds to convert it from a shipping container to a medical cabinet. It is designed specifically to keep life saving equipment/supplies safe and secure from moisture, dust, sand, temperature and impact, and includes one sliding shelf that is designed for medical supplies such as blood pressure cuffs and stethoscopes. This rotationally-molded, airtight case features drawers (measuring 16” x 14¾” x 6½” each) that are removable and interchangeable. It has a top sliding shelf with mesh and a Velcro closure, along with a pocket in the door. It has built-in wheels, is chemical agent decontaminable and stackable.

### Technology & Features

-

Spring loaded butterfly latches for easy opening.

-

Airtight, waterproof, sand proof and chemical agent decontaminable.

-

One sliding shelf on top. Each of the 3 drawers is 16” x 14¾” x 6½”.

### Additional Features:

* Lid doubles as a door, and has an attached zippered pouch for X-rays or any odd-shaped items.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Closed:** 34″ x 21″ x 19″
**Weight:** 39 lbs.
