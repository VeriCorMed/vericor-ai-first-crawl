# Oxygen Distribution (for Large DUV)

**Summary:** An oxygen distribution kit used to deliver bed-side oxygen to patients within a large Dual Use Vehicle (DUV). Module has a compact footprint and can rapidly be deployed. (Made specifically for the Dept. of Veterans Affairs.)

The Oxygen Distribution for Large DUV (SS-DUV-O2LG) is a kit for delivering bed-side oxygen to patients within a large Dual Use Vehicle (DUV). This kit, specifically made for the Dept. of Veterans Affairs, has a compact footprint and can rapidly be deployed. All components are contained within a wheeled, labeled, protective case for safe storage and easy movement. The cascade system and manifold attach to the L-track within the DUV for rapid set-up and safe oxygen distribution. It has five oxygen cascade systems (one per litter station), each with three patient drops (one per litter), along with one oxygen manifold accommodating up to eight patients. A SmartBook™ containing photos and critical data also included.

### Technology & Features

-

8-patient manifold attached to the L-track within a DUV for secure oxygen distribution.

-

Five oxygen cascade systems (3 drops each) attached to the L-track within a DUV.

-

Components packaged within a mobile Medical Equipment Response Case.

### Additional Features

* SmartBook™ system guide aides in deployment and training.
