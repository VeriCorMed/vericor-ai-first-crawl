# Laboratory

**Summary:** A medical kit loaded with components needed for performing phlebotomy and point-of-care testing (POCT). This emergency preparedness MedKit has medical supplies organized into the pockets of a jump bag for those disaster response scenarios.

The Laboratory medical kit (MK-L) is a jump bag for performing phlebotomy and point-of-care testing (POCT), including Strep A, pregnancy and diabetes. Supplies include glucometer, test strips, specimen cups, etc. Prepackaged into an Upright Medical Organizer (MC-UMO), supplies are intuitively packaged and ready to use. Roll it open and hang it on the included easy to assemble stand (MC-SUMO). Store it in your choice of case: MC-1 (hard case) or MC-BUMO (soft case). A SmartBook™ containing photos and critical data also included.

### Technology & Features

-

Highly visible ID holder identifies type of MedKit.

-

Clear pockets provide users visibility of the supplies.

-

Collapsible stand snaps together in under a minute.

### Additional Features

* Choose the packaging. Either the UMO is rolled up and put into a Wheeled Medical Case (MC-1) or a Protective Carry Bag (MC-BUMO).
* Pre-attached handle for carrying when the MedKit is rolled up.

### Dimensions & Specs

**On Stand:**73″ x 39″ x 25″ @ 91 lbs.
**Soft Case (stored):** 30” x 15” x 15”
**Hard Case (stored):** 38” x 23” x 23”
