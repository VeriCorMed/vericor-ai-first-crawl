# ICP PPE Kit (Medium)

**Summary:** A prepackaged kit of personal protective equipment (PPE), size medium. This equipment is worn to minimize exposure to a variety of hazards. Ideal for healthcare and support staff in contact with suspected, or confirmed, infectious disease patients.

The Infection Control and Prevention PPE Kit, Medium (SS-ICPK-MD) provides ready to use PPE for healthcare and support staff who will be in contact with a suspected, or confirmed, infectious disease patient. This single kit contains medium-sized PPE gear in a small, compact package. It includes extended cuff nitrile gloves, an impervious coverall, N95 mask, face shield, hood (head and neck cover), boot covers, apron, and more. CDC compliant for Ebola as of 3/1/2020.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Kit:** 14” x 9” x 6”
**Weight:** 2 lbs
