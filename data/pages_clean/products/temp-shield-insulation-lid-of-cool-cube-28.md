# Temp-Shield™ Insulation Lid of Cool Cube™ 28

**Summary:** A vacuum insulation panel that acts as the lid of the Cool Cube™ 28. This panel provides advanced thermal protection for temperature sensitive product by utilizing vacuum insulation technology for the lid of the cooler.

The Temp-Shield™ Insulation Lid of Cool Cube™ 28 (CC-VIPLID-28) is a replacement lid for the original insulation system of the Cool Cube™ 28. The lid panel is a rigid board of powder, shrink wrapped, encased with an aluminum membrane skin, and sealed under vacuum.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Lid only.**
**Outside:** 16” x 16” x 1”
Weight: 2 lbs.
