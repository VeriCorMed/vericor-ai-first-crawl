# Laryngoscope Kit Organizer

**Summary:** A carrying case that stores and protects laryngoscope handles, blades, ET tubes or anything that needs protection and organization. The classic attaché style is made for carrying, while the clear outer pocket is great for custom identification.

The Laryngoscope Kit Organizer (MC-LKO) stores and protects laryngoscope handles, blades, ET tubes and forceps, or anything that needs protection and organization. The classic attaché style is made for carrying, while the clear outer pocket is great for custom identification. When open, the padded case spreads completely open for quick access to the contents. On the left side of the case there are two pockets of equal size. On the right side of the case there are four pockets of varying height. It measures 15.5" x 11" x 1" and weighs 0.5 lb.

**Closed:** 14” x 10” x 1”
**Weight:** 1 lb.
