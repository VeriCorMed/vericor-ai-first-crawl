# Rolling Rack for Beds (customized for 1-30 beds)

**Summary:** A rolling storage and deployment rack customized for various sized beds and/or quantities. This rack is ideal for storing and deploying beds made by other manufacturers, and is constructed according to customer specifications (limit of 30 beds).

The Rolling Rack for Beds (RR-CBR) is a built-to-specs storage and deployment rack for surge beds. This rack is constructed according to customer specifications with heavy-duty steel and a baked-on, powder-coat finish to protect it from dings and scratches during exercises and/or deployment. Four wheels (two swivel and two fixed) ensure dynamic maneuverability and ease of movement on almost any surface. The rack features a welded-steel construction with laser-cut components for no sharp edges. Let this custom rack support the beds (no more than 30) needed for those emergency preparation and mitigation plans. Measurements will vary according to specifications given by the customer.

### Technology & Features

-

Welded steel construction with laser cut components with no sharp edges.

-

Baked-on, powder-coating finish.

-

Two fixed and two swivel, 3" diameter wheels.
