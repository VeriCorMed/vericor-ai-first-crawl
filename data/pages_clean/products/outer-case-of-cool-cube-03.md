# Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 03

**Summary:** An outer case that snugly fits the Temp-Shield™ Insulation System to create the portable Cool Cube™ 03 PCM cooler. This fabric bag is a standard blue with a carrying handle, shoulder strap, clear pockets and double slide zipper. *(VIP & PCM Panels not included.)*

The Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 03 (CC-Case-03) is a soft-sided bag made up of a lightweight, durable material, that fits the Temp-Shield™ vacuum insulated panel (VIP) system of Cool Cube™ 03 models. The case features an adjustable, padded shoulder strap with a built-in carrying handle, clear outer pockets and a double-zipper design. This case is specifically made to make transport easy.

### Technology & Features

 **![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-1-150x150.png)Straps**

An adjustable, padded shoulder strap eases longer commutes and is removable. The fixed carrying handle is great for general use and moving of product.

**![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-2-150x150.png)Zippers**

The double zipper closure has toggles for easy access. Zipper design also enables users to close lid securely around an external-to-internal monitor probe cord.

**![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-3-150x150.png)Pockets**

Clear, external pockets are on all four sides and are great for digital data loggers (DDLs), paperwork, pens and any other necessary items needed to go with the product.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Outside:** 11″ x 11″ x 11″
**Weight:** 1 lb.
