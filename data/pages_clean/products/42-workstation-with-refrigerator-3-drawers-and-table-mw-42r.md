# 42” Workstation w/ 3 Drawers, Table & Refrigerator

**Summary:** A mobile workstation with three drawers for storage, a refrigerator for vaccine (or other temperature sensitive product), and a detachable table for working on. Undo a couple latches and it opens up, ready for work.

The 42” Workstation w/ 3 Drawers, Table & Refrigerator (MC-42R) is a mobile workstation for medical supply storage and mobility. This self-contained case on wheels has ample storage, a built-in refrigerator, transport handles and a detachable table that can move when and where it is needed. It is lockable for secure storage, has an adjustable refrigerator without freezer, glove holders in the lid for easy access, and easy lock wheels with a wide wheel base for easy/safe maneuverability.

### Technology & Features

-

Refrigerator has an adjustable thermostat (no freezer compartment) and is lockable for secure storage.

-

Removable lid can be turned into a table for an additional work surface.

-

Stainless steel drawers are deep with customizable dividers.

### Additional Features

* Easy lock wheels have a wide wheel base for easy and safe maneuverability.
* Lid stays for the top cover assure safety.
* Four recessed handles for ease of movement.

* Two spring-loaded recessed tie downs raise for application and lay down when not in use.
* Butterfly latches for secure transport.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Closed:** 48” x 43” x 25”
**Weight:** 283 lbs.
