# Controlled Room Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 96

**Summary:** A set of six panels containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. These panels for the Cool Cube™ 96 provide advanced thermal protection for room temp product due to a 21.5°C/70.7°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Controlled Room Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 96 (6 Panels) (CC-PCMS-R96) is used to create a 15° to 25°C or 20° to 24°C environment within Cool Cube™ 96 models. This phase change material system of panels can be utilized to transition any Cool Cube™ 96 into one to manage room temperature product – ideal for FFPE, platelets and biospecimens.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Stacked (storage):** 19¼” x 19¼” x 6”
**Assembled Inside Pack-Out Space:** 18” x 18” x 18”
**Weight:** 49 lbs.
