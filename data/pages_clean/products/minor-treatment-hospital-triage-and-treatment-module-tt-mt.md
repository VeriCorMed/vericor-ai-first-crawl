The Minor Treatment Hospital Triage & Treatment Module provides the components necessary for medical staff to care for casualties who have minor injuries. This piece of the complete Triage & Treatment System is utilized at the scene of an accident or mass-casualty incident. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly. It includes 1 Rolling Chest, 1 Medical Organizer w/ 4 Drawers & Table, 4 Upright Medical Organizers, and supplies/equipment to address the following care functions: staff personal protective equipment, examination, orthopedics, wound care, and wound closure.
