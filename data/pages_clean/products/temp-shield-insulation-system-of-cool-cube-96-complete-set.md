# Temp-Shield™ Insulation System of Cool Cube™ 96 (Full Set)

**Summary:** A set of six vacuum insulation panels that form the barrier of the Cool Cube™ 96. These panels provide advanced thermal protection for temperature sensitive product by utilizing vacuum insulation technology on all six sides of the cooler.

The Temp-Shield™ Insulation System of Cool Cube™ 96 (Complete Set) (CC-VIP-96) consists of six (6) panels sealed together to form the replaceable insulating barrier of Cool Cube™ 96 models. Each panel is a rigid board of powder, shrink wrapped, encased with an aluminum membrane skin, and sealed under vacuum.

### Technology & Features

- **5X More Efficient**

Cool Cube™ vacuum insulation panel (VIP) technology has a high level of insulation performance with very low thermal conductivity, enabling a thin design. This means more room for payload and less bulk to carry.

- **Long Life**

Quality core material wrapped in a tough aluminum membrane skin extends the life expectancy of these VIPs well past 5 years. Testing results indicate panels are 95% effective after 5 years and can last 10-20 years. VIPs are replaceable.

- **Added Protection**

A clear protective laminate withstands the rigors of everyday use. This clear layers lets users inspect the panel(s) for signs of vacuum loss while providing an extra film of protection for the skin.
