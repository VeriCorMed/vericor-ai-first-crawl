The Hospital Triage & Treatment Module provides the components necessary for medical staff to determine priorities for who gets care first by sorting injured or sick people according to their need for emergency medical attention. This piece ofthe complete Triage & Treatment System is utilized at the scene of an accident or mass-casualty incident. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly. It includes 1 Rolling Chest, 4 Backboards, Triage Tags, Cervical Collars, First Aid Kits, Litters, and more.
