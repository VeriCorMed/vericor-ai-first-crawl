# Fridge Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 08 (6 Panels)

**Summary:** A set of six panels containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. These panels for the Cool Cube™ 08 provide advanced thermal protection for refrigerated product due to a 4.5°C/40.1°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Fridge Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 08 (6 Panels) (CC-PCMS-BV08) is used to create a 2° to 8°C, 1° to 10°C, or 1° to 6°C environment within Cool Cube™ 08 models. This phase change material system of panels can be utilized to transition any Cool Cube™ 08 into one to manage refrigerator temperatures – ideal for fresh vaccine, medicine and blood products.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Stacked (storage):** 12¼” x 7¾” x 6”
**Assembled Inside Pack-Out Space:** 11¼” x 6½” x 6½”
**Weight:** 8½ lbs.
