The Orthopedics ACS Module provides the essential items necessary to stabilize and treat injuries of the musculoskeletal system. This ACS (Alternate Care Site) module is utilized during a patient surge in the wake of a disaster. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly. It includes 1 Upright Medical Organizer (UMO) within a Primary Medical Case, 1 Additional Primary Medical Case, a Cast Saw, Pro Splint Kits, Scotchcast Splints, Ring Cutter, Elastic & Triangle Bandages, Cold Packs, and more.
