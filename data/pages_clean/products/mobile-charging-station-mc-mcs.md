# Mobile Charging Station

**Summary:** A unique equipment chest with built-in electrical and shelving for charging EP supplies. Doors swing open to reveal built-in door shelving and main compartment wire shelving. Keep equipment organized and fully charged with this exclusive case.

The Mobile Charging Station (MC-MCS) is a unique equipment chest for keeping medical equipment organized and fully charged. With built-in electrical, this durable case protects assets during storage and deployment, while providing a lockable solution that organizes charging stations and power supplies. Pack it up and be ready to respond. It features carriage doors with shelves, seven swivel casters (2 locking), an overhead light, premium power strips, wire shelving, and bottom shelf tie downs.

### Technology & Features

-

Carriage doors have built in shelves.

-

Premium power strips and each level.

-

7 swivel caster (4") of which two are locking.

### Additional Features

* Shelving tie downs.
* Two spring-loaded recessed tie downs that raise for application and lay down when not in use.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Closed:** 55″ x 49″ x 26″
**Open:** 55″ x 98″ x 26″
**Weight:** 315 lbs.
