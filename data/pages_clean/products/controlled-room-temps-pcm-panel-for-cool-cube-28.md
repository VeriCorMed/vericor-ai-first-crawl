# Controlled Room Temps PCM Panel for Cool Cube™ 28

**Summary:** A panel containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. This single panel for the Cool Cube™ 28 provides advanced thermal protection for room temperature product due to a 21.5°C/70.7°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Controlled Room Temps PCM Panel for Cool Cube™ 28 (CC-PCMP-R28) is a reusable panel used to create a 15° to 25°C or 20° to 24°C environment within Cool Cube™ 28 models. This phase change material panel is used to manage room temperature product – ideal for FFPE, platelets and biospecimens.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Outside:** 13¼” x 13¼” x 1”
**Inside Edge:** 12” x 12”
**Weight:** 3½ lbs.
