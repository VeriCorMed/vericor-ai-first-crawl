# Fridge Temps PCM Panel for Cool Cube™ 03

**Summary:** A panel containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. This single panel for the Cool Cube™ 03 provides advanced thermal protection for refrigerated product due to a 4.5°C/40.1°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Fridge Temps PCM Panel for Cool Cube™ 03 (CC-PCMP-BV03) is a reusable panel used to create a 2° to 8°C, 1° to 10°C, or 1° to 6°C environment within Cool Cube™ 03 models. This phase change material panel is used to manage refrigerator temperatures – ideal for fresh vaccine, medicine and blood products.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Outside:** 7¼” x 7¼” x 1”
**Inside Edge:** 6” x 6”
**Weight:** 1 lb.
