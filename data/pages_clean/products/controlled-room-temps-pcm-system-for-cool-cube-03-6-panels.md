# Controlled Room Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 03

**Summary:** A set of six panels containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. These panels for the Cool Cube™ 03 provide advanced thermal protection for room temp product due to a 21.5°C/70.7°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Controlled Room Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 03 (6 Panels) (CC-PCMS-R03) is used to create a 15° to 25°C or 20° to 24°C environment within Cool Cube™ 03 models. This phase change material system of panels can be utilized to transition any Cool Cube™ 03 into one to manage room temperature product – ideal for FFPE, platelets and biospecimens.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Stacked (storage):** 7¼” x 7¼” x 6”
**Assembled Inside Pack-Out Space:** 6” x 6” x 6”
**Weight:** 6 lbs.
