# Active Patient Care Bed w/ IV Pole & Rails

**Summary:** A medical surge bed for continuous patient care. This 28” tall (procedure height) bed presents patients at a comfortable height for healthcare professionals. The stow-on-board IV pole and safety rails fold up with the rest of the bed.

The Active Patient Care Bed w/ IV Pole & Rails (RB-APC400ISR) is a medical surge bed for continuous patient care. This taller, procedure height bed allows healthcare professionals to preform necessary functions at a working height. In the event of an overflow, the APC doubles effectively as a temporary cot. It is lightweight and portable, has a five position head elevator, two position foot elevator, anti-microbial mattress, spring and deck design, patient restraint belt, side rails, and a stow on board IV pole. With a 450 pound working capacity, the sleeping surface is 32″ x 80″ at a height of 28″. This bed meets FED STD 1633 for flammability.

### Technology & Features

-

2” convoluted foam mattress on top of a spring and deck design.

-

Heavy-duty leg locks on all legs.

-

Breaks down and stores away in a compact footprint.

### Additional Features

* Two position Trendelenburg foot elevator.
* Five position head elevator with locking cord.
* Stow on board IV pole and side rails included.

### Dimensions & Specs

**In Use:** 80” x 32” x 28”
**Folded (storage):** 34” x 42” x 10”
**Weight:** 43 lbs.
