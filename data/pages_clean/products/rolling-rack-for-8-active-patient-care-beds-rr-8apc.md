# Rolling Rack for 8 APC Beds

**Summary:** A rolling storage and deployment rack for 8 Active Patient Care Beds. This heavy duty steel rack has 4 shelves along with fixed and swivel casters for easy movement on most surfaces.

The Rolling Rack for 8 APC Beds (RR-8APC) is a rolling storage and deployment rack for 8 Active Patient Care Beds. This rack is constructed of heavy-duty steel with a baked-on, powder-coat finish to protect it from dings and scratches during exercises and/or deployment. Four wheels (two swivel and two fixed) ensure dynamic maneuverability and ease of movement on almost any surface. The rack features a welded-steel construction with laser-cut components for no sharp edges. Let these four shelves support the beds needed for those emergency preparation and mitigation plans.

### Technology & Features

-

Welded steel construction with laser cut components with no sharp edges.

-

Baked-on, powder-coating finish.

-

Two fixed and two swivel, 3" diameter wheels.

**H x D x W:** 80” x 43” x 38”
**Weight:** 148 lbs.
