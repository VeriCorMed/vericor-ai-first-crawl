# Decon Equipment Organizer

**Summary:** A large equipment rack that has multiple compartments to protect bulky decontamination equipment during storage and deployment. The welded steel construction has no sharp edges and is ideal for the protection of medical assets.

The Decon Equipment Organizer (MC-DEO) is a large equipment rack that protects decontamination equipment during storage and enables the mass movement/deployment of bulk supplies when needed. Large casters enable this organizer to move easily on carpet, vinyl, wood and concrete flooring. The welded steel construction has no sharp edges and is ideal for the protection of medical assets. It features three open shelves with a heavy-duty vinyl “door”, two steering handles, four swivel casters (two are locking), and a see-through design.

### Technology & Features

-

1.25” square steel tubing with a powder-coating finish.

-

Heavy-duty vinyl door.

-

6” diameter wheels.

**Closed:** 65″ x 63½″ x 30″
