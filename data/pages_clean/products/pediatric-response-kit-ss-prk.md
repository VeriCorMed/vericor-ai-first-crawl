# Pediatric Response Kit

**Summary:** A medical kit loaded with components needed to care for pediatric emergencies. This emergency preparedness, mass casualty incident, medical kit augments facilities with unique supplies and equipment necessary to care for pediatric patients.

The Pediatric Response Kit (SS-PRK) is a module of the unique supplies and equipment for pediatric care. This kit has a compact footprint and can rapidly be deployed when time is of the essence. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly™. All items are contained within a wheeled case for easy storage and rapid deployment including pediatric airways and tubes, IV catheters, splints, collars, and more. A SmartBook™ containing photos and critical data is also included.

### Technology & Features

-

Prepackaged pediatric advanced life support (ALS) kit with supplies.

-

Deluxe nylon pack is organized with other large supplies into a Compact Response Case (MC-CRC).

-

SmartBook™ system guide aides in deployment and training.
