# 42” Workstation w/ 6 Drawers & Table

**Summary:** A mobile workstation with six drawers for storage, plus a detachable table for working on. Undo a couple latches & it opens up, ready for work. This self-contained case is ideal for in-field clinics needing to store & transport medical supplies.

The 42” Workstation w/ 6 Drawers & Table (MC-42) is a mobile workstation for medical supply storage and mobility. This self-contained case on wheels has ample storage, transport handles and a detachable table that can move when and where it is needed. It is lockable for secure storage, has glove holders in the lid for easy access, and easy lock wheels with a wide wheel base for easy/safe maneuverability.

### Technology & Features

-

Removable lid can be turned into a table for an additional work surface.

-

Stainless steel drawers are deep with customizable dividers.

-

Easy lock wheels have a wide wheel base for easy and safe maneuverability.

### Additional Features

* Lid stays for the top cover assure safety.
* Four recessed handles for ease of movement.

* Two spring-loaded recessed tie downs raise for application and lay down when not in use.
* Butterfly latches for secure transport.

### Dimensions & Specs

**Closed:** 48” x 43” x 25”
**Weight:** 296 lbs.
