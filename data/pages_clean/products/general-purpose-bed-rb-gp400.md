# General-Purpose Bed

**Summary:** A general use cot ready to take the abuse of any shelter, day in and day out. This lightweight, durable bed is comfortable and portable, making it an excellent choice for the general population and staff needs.

The General-Purpose Bed (RB-GP400) is a medical surge bed for the general population and support staff. This lightweight, portable cot is great for the rigors of long or short term deployment. It has spun tube ends and an anti-microbial, waterproof mattress, with a comfortable spring and deck design. With a 450 pound working capacity, the sleeping surface is 32″ x 80″ at a height of 22″. This bed meets FED STD 1633 for flammability and is ADA compliant.

### Technology & Features

-

2” convoluted foam mattress on top of a spring and deck design.

-

Heavy-duty leg locks on all legs.

-

Breaks down and stores away in a compact footprint.

### Additional Features

* Mattress meets FED STD 1633 for flammability and is ADA compliant.
* Waterproof, antimicrobial, soil resistant mattress.

### Dimensions & Specs

**In Use:** 80” x 32” x 22”
**Folded (storage):** 42” x 34” x 4½”
**Weight:** 18 lbs.
