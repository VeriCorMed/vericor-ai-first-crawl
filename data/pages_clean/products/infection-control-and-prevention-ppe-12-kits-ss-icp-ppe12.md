# Infection Control and Prevention PPE (12 Kits)

**Summary:** A prepackaged module of 12 personal protective equipment (PPE) kits. This equipment is worn to minimize exposure to a variety of hazards. Ideal for healthcare and support staff in contact with suspected, or confirmed, infectious disease patients.

The Infection Control and Prevention PPE (12 Kits) Specialized Response Module provides basic, ready to use PPE kits for healthcare and support staff who will be in contact with a suspected, or confirmed, infectious disease patient. This Response-Friendly module contains individually packaged kits of PPE (according to size) that is intuitively organized and clearly labeled. It includes 1 tote, extended cuff nitrile gloves, impervious coveralls, N95 masks, face shields, hoods (head and neck covers), boot covers, aprons, and more. CDC compliant for Ebola as of 3/1/2020.

### Technology & Features

-

Includes a fold-over tote with prepackaged kits.

-

Kit sizes are clearly labeled.

-

Kit sizes: 2 small, 4 medium, 4 large and 2 extra large.
