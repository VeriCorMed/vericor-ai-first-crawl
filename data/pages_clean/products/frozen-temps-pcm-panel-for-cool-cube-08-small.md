# Lab Freezer Temps PCM Panel for Cool Cube™ 08, Small

**Summary:** A panel containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. This single, smaller panel for the Cool Cube™ 03 provides advanced thermal protection for frozen product due to a -21.5°C/-6.7°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Lab Freezer Temps PCM Panel for Cool Cube™ 08, Small (CC-PCMP-F08-S) is a reusable panel used to create a -50° to -15°C environment within Cool Cube™ 08 models. This phase change material panel is used to manage frozen product – ideal for frozen vaccine such as varicella, MMRV and zoster, as well as Fresh Frozen Plasma (FFP). NOTE: An ultra-low freezer capable of holding a -25°C/-13°F or lower temperature is needed to prep the panel for maximum hold time.

**Outside:** 7¾” x 7¾” x 1”
**Inside Edge:** 6½” x 6½”
