# Cool Cube™ Transport Cart

**Summary:** Lightweight, foldable transport cart for the Cool Cube™ 03 and Cool Cube™ 08. With a compact design for easy storage and portability, it opens in seconds to create a sturdy cart to roll around Cool Cubes™ safely and efficiently.

Wheel a Cool Cube™ PCM Cooler around with this lightweight, foldable Cool Cube™ Transport Cart (CC-TC). This cart folds down into a compact design for easy storage, yet opens in seconds to safely roll a Cool Cube™ 03, 08, or a combination of Cool Cubes™ during transport. A large, flat area combined with an adjustable bungee system stabilizes loads. Meanwhile, two oversized rugged wheels work in harmony with the sturdy handle to enable precise handling.

 **![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-1-150x150.png)Telescoping Handle**

Adjustable height pull handle.

**![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-2-150x150.png)Bungee System**

**![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-3-150x150.png)Two Oversized Wheels**

Rugged wheels can support 77 lbs.
