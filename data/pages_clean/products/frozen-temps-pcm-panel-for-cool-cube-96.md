# Lab Freezer Temps PCM Panel for Cool Cube™ 96

**Summary:** A panel containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. This single panel for the Cool Cube™ 96 provides advanced thermal protection for frozen product due to a -21.5°C/-6.7°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Lab Freezer Temps PCM Panel for Cool Cube™ 96 (CC-PCMP-F96) is a reusable panel used to create a -50° to -15°C environment within Cool Cube™ 96 models. This phase change material panel is used to manage frozen product – ideal for frozen vaccine such as varicella, MMRV and zoster, as well as Fresh Frozen Plasma (FFP). NOTE: An ultra-low freezer capable of holding a -25°C/-13°F or lower temperature is needed to prep the panel for maximum hold time.

**Inside Edge:** 18” x 18”
