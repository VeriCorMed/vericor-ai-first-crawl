The Emergency Care Alternate Care Site Module (ACS-EC) Module provides the components necessary to manage arrest situations, immobilization, OB delivery and traction. This ACS (Alternate Care Site) module is utilized during a patient surge in the wake of a disaster. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly™. It includes 2 Primary Medical Cases, an AED Defibrillator w/ Protective Case, Pediatric ALS Kit, Emergency Oxygen Kit, Backboards, Splints, Patient Movers, and more.
