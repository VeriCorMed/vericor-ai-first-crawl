# Tru-Trac System™ of Cool Cube™ 28 (Complete Set)

**Summary:** A wheel kit for the older Cool Cube™ 28 models that didn’t have the current Tru-Trac System™ of mobility (mounting hardware included).

The Tru-Trac System™ of Cool Cube™ 28 (CC-CWK) includes four heavy duty caster-style wheels (two locking/swivel and two fixed) with mounting hardware.
