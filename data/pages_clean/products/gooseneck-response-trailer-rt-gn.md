# Gooseneck Response Trailer

**Summary:** **Features Include**

* Insulated Vinyl Walls & Ceiling
* E-track in Floor and Walls
* Custom Decal and Labeling
* Roof Mounted A/C and Heat
* Interior & Exterior Lighting
* 4’ Foot Base Cabinet w/ Drawer System
* Available in lengths from 36’ to 53’
* 8.5’ Wide
* 7’Interior Height
* Tandem or Triple axle
* etc.

The Gooseneck Response Trailer is ready to respond with strength, multi-functionality and style. This tandem axle hauler is quality driven and built to handle the movement and deployment of equipment and supplies quickly and efficiently. The speed, capability and flexibility of this trailer can improve outcomes by enabling a quick response. At 8.5 feet wide, this unit comes standard with an interior height of 7 feet (6" taller than typical trailers), insulated vinyl walls and ceiling, a 4 foot base cabinet with drawer system, E-track in the floor and walls, interior and exterior lighting, an electric tongue jack, roof mounted A/C and heat, and more.
