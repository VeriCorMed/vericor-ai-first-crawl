# Lab Freezer Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 96 (6 Panels)

**Summary:** A set of six panels containing phase change material (PCM) that is nontoxic & biodegradable. These panels for the Cool Cube™ 96 provide advanced thermal protection for frozen product due to a -21.5°C/-6.7°F melting point (temperature hold).

The Lab Freezer Temps PCM System for Cool Cube™ 96 (6 Panels) (CC-PCMS-F96) is used to create a -50° to -15°C environment within Cool Cube™ 96 models. This phase change material system of panels can be utilized to transition any Cool Cube™ 96 into one to manage frozen product – ideal for frozen vaccine such as varicella, MMRV and zoster, as well as Fresh Frozen Plasma (FFP). NOTE: An ultra-low freezer capable of holding a -25°C/-13°F or lower temperature is needed to prep the panels for maximum hold time.
