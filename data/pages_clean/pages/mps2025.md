# Medical Product Specialist
![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/med-prod-spec-de2.jpg)
**Join a Leading Innovator in Mobile Medical Solutions for Emergency and Disaster Response  
** Are you a detail-oriented individual with a passion for quality and a desire to contribute to a mission that truly matters? At VeriCor, we’re at the forefront of developing innovative mobile medical solutions that empower healthcare professionals to save lives in critical situations nationwide. We’re seeking a dedicated and skilled **Medical Product Specialist** to join our growing team and play a vital role in bringing these life-saving products to those who need them most.
In this multifaceted role, you’ll be more than just an assembler. You’ll be a crucial part of the entire product lifecycle, from our emergency medical products’ precise assembly and quality assurance to providing valuable technical insights and collaborating on developing our next-generation Response-Friendly™ solutions. We encourage you to apply if you have a strong work ethic, technical aptitude, and a genuine commitment to producing high-quality medical products for public health and hospitals.
**What You’ll Do:**
  * Participate in the meticulous assembly and packaging of emergency medical products, adhering to strict quality standards and work instructions.
  * Efficiently prepare and manage shipments of finished goods to fulfill customer orders accurately and on time.
  * Collaborate closely with engineering and product development, providing valuable technical feedback and support to enhance our products.
  * Develop and maintain bills of material and work instructions to ensure efficient and consistent production processes.
  * Conduct quality assurance checks to guarantee that all products meet our specifications and performance standards.
  * Utilize our Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system effectively for relevant tasks, inventory management, and data accuracy.
  * Engage with colleagues and customers to gather valuable feedback, contributing directly to the innovation and development of our next-generation Response-Friendly™ products.

**What You’ll Bring:**
  * Demonstrated experience working with emergency medical products and a solid understanding of their critical functionality.
  * Outstanding manual dexterity and strong spatial reasoning abilities.
  * Proficiency in basic mathematics and Microsoft Excel; comfort and adaptability with new software systems.
  * Physical capability to perform required tasks, including routine lifting of up to 20 lbs and occasional lifting of up to 80 lbs.
  * A proactive and eager approach to learning new skills and a genuine desire for professional growth within our organization.

**Bonus Points For:**
  * Practical “handyman” skills with hands-on experience in mechanical assembly and repair.
  * Excellent verbal and written communication skills, enabling effective collaboration and information sharing.
  * Relevant technical certifications or an associate’s or bachelor’s degree in a related field (commensurate experience will also be strongly considered).

**Why Choose VeriCor?**  
At VeriCor, you’re not just an employee but a vital contributor to our success and a vital part of a team dedicated to saving lives. We offer a collaborative and supportive environment where your contributions are valued, and your professional growth is encouraged. We’re proud to offer:
  * **Competitive Compensation:** A salary range of $40,000-$70,000, commensurate with experience and qualifications, plus profit-sharing opportunities.
  * **Generous Time Off:** Accrued Paid Time Off (PTO) to ensure a healthy work-life balance.
  * **Flexible Work:** A flexible in-office work schedule to accommodate individual needs.
  * **Health Insurance Benefits:** Access to health insurance options to support your well-being.
  * **Savings for Healthcare:** Health Savings Account (HSA) and Flexible Spending Account (FSA) options to help manage healthcare costs.
  * **Secure Your Future:** A SIMPLE IRA retirement plan with company matching to help you plan for your long-term financial security.
  * **Make a Real Impact:** Be part of a company making a tangible difference in healthcare, providing critical emergency and disaster response solutions.
