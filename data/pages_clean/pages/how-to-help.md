# How to Help
![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/header-help.png)
VeriCor is a leading provider of critical infrastructure solutions that help organizations and communities proactively prepare for and mitigate the impact of natural disasters. We specialize in developing innovative technologies and services that enhance resilience and ensure a rapid recovery process. By investing in VeriCor’s solutions, you’re taking a proactive step towards safeguarding your assets, protecting lives, and building a more resilient future.
![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/hurricane-300x200.jpg)
In the wake of Hurricanes Helene and Milton, communities in the affected regions are grappling with the urgent task of rebuilding and recovery. The extensive damage caused by these storms has left countless individuals and families displaced and in need of essential support. At VeriCor, we are dedicated to providing resources and guidance for those seeking to contribute to the relief efforts.
We have compiled a list of reputable organizations and volunteer opportunities (in alphabetical order) where your generosity, whether in the form of time, resources, or financial contributions, can make a significant difference in the lives of those affected by these natural disasters.
