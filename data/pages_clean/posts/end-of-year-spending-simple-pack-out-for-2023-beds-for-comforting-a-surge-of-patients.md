### C) Cold Chain Data Loggers
#### VFC Compliant
The cold chain is about controlling the environment of temperature-sensitive products so that the product is in optimal condition when needed. Exposure to any highs or lows results in significant problems. The CDC recommends using a specific temperature monitoring device called a Digital Data Logger (DDL). A DDL continuously monitors and records data points for review later. VeriCor carries a line of simple and affordable DDLs.
