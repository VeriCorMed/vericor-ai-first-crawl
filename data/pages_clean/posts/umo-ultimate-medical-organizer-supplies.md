# An UMO …the ultimate medical organizer for supplies
Every VA medical center* has two of these. What is it and what can it do for you?[![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/ultimate-medical-organizer-230x1024.jpg)](https://www.vericormed.com/product/upright-medical-organizer-mc-umo-medical-roll-pack/)

An “UMO” (Upright Medical Organizer) is a medical roll pack used to organize supplies. When hung vertically, it provides rapid, easy access to medical supplies utilizing clear, partitioned pockets. When rolled up (for storage or transport), it provides secure, stay-in-place organization utilizing zippers and straps.

For fast and easy access to supplies, simply roll-and-go with the UMO.

Features:

    * EZ Hang System™ (built-in hooks and straps to securely hang on doors, shelter frames and stands)
    * Heavy duty, polyester fabric coated with blue, washable poly vinyl chloride body
    * 20 transparent, UV inhibited vinyl pockets
    * 4 glove (with cut outs), 15 supply, 1 oversize
    * Structural reinforcement rods
    * Double zippered pockets
    * Straps to secure rolled up UMO
    * Handle for carrying rolled up UMO
    * Identification label holder on top

Specs:

  * 67” x 26”
  * 7 lbs.

Additional Accessories

  * [Stand (free-standing)](https://www.vericormed.com/product/stand-for-upright-medical-organizer-mc-sumo/) – a quick and secure assembly utilizing a Fast-Lock™ connection system.
  * [Protective Bag](https://www.vericormed.com/product/protective-carry-bag-for-mc-umo-mc-bumo/) – safeguard and carry a packed-out UMO with ease.

Related Products

  * [MedKits](https://www.vericormed.com/medkits/) – pre-packaged medical kits containing supplies focused on a specific care function.

*All VA Medical Centers that own a Dual-Use Vehicle (a patient transportation bus that converts into a patient evacuation vehicle) have a VeriCor medical supply kit packed-out and ready to respond to emergencies.
