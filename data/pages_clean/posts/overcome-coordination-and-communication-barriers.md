### Overcome Coordination/Communication Barriers
#### A FREE platform for all EMS & healthcare organizations.
_This is not an affiliate nor does VeriCor have any connection to this company—it’s just a nice gesture by this company that we wanted to recognize and spread the word about to those of you that might find it useful._
Pulsara is offering its HIPAA-compliant, video-enabled platform for FREE during the COVID-19 pandemic (unlimited number of users). By facilitating networked communication (live video and messaging) across organizations and teams during this pandemic, [Pulsara](https://www.pulsara.com/covid-19) can:
  * 1. Mitigate patient surges.
  * 2. Limit transfer cases.
  * 3. Minimize exposures.
  * 4. Facilitate PPE conservation.
  * 5. Secure communication for outpatients in quarantine.

For more information or to get the free package visit: <https://www.pulsara.com/covid-19>
