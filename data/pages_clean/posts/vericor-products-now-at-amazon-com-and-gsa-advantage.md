# VeriCor products now at Amazon.com and GSA Advantage
### HOLMEN, WI – June 3, 2015 – VeriCor, LLC, producer of Response-Friendly™ Medical Systems, announces the addition of online marketplaces [Amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com/s/ref=sr_nr_p_89_0?fst=as%3Aoff&rh=i%3Aaps%2Ck%3Avericor%2Cp_89%3AVeriCor&keywords=vericor&ie=UTF8&qid=1435156035&rnid=2528832011) and GSA Advantage!
“Customers want an easy way to research and buy products online” said Luther Olson, VeriCor’s Marketing Director. “[Amazon.com](https://amzn.to/1SgoLG5) and GSA Advantage do just that – they provide an environment customers are comfortable with, making it easier and faster to obtain Response-Friendly™ products.”
- Public Health Departments, hospitals, clinics, VA’s, and individuals with Amazon accounts are able to purchase these products with a click of a button at[ Amazon.com](https://amzn.to/1SgoLG5):
  * [Cool Cubes™ ](https://www.vericormed.com/product-category/cool-cube-transport-coolers/)– Cold-chain management coolers for the transport and storage of vaccines (fresh and frozen), blood products (fresh and FFP), and other temperature sensitive products.
  * Certified Thermometers – Calibrated, digital thermometers with a glycol-encased probe (recommended by the CDC).
  * [Medical Response Beds](https://www.vericormed.com/response-beds/) – Designed for in-field patient treatment.
  * [Upright Medical Organizers](https://www.vericormed.com/product/upright-medical-organizer-mc-umo/) – Organize, roll-up and transport medical supplies.
