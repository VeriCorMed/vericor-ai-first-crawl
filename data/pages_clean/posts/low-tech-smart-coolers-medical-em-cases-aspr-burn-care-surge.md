### Low-Tech Smart Coolers![Coolers for Vaccine Blood and Pharmaceuticles](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Coolers-for-Vaccine-Blood-and-Pharmaceuticles-300x200.jpg)
#### for Vaccine, Blood, Pharmaceuticals
  * No cumbersome cords to manage.
  * No moving parts to break.
  * No frozen ice bottles (0°C) to condition.
  * No cheap gel packs to disintegrate.

These simple, safe, dependable coolers have 3 parts: a case, the insulation, and the temperature cartridges (flat PCM panels that surround the contents at the correct temperature…4°C, -20°C, or 22°C). Cool Cubes™ are simple, safe and low-tech.

#### On-Site Patient Treatment
Burn care is a priority within ASPR’s 2017-2022 Health Care Preparedness and Response Capabilities.
