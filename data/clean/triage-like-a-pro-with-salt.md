### A) Facing a Mass Casualty Incident?
#### Triage Like a Pro with SALT
The SALT method is a four-step process that helps manage chaotic situations.
SALT stands for:
  1. **Sort:** Quickly categorize patients based on their mobility.
  2. **Assess:** Evaluate patients‚Äô conditions and administer lifesaving interventions within seconds.
  3. **Lifesaving interventions:** Perform critical actions like stopping severe bleeding or opening airways.
  4. **Treatment/Transport:** Prioritize treatment and transport based on the severity of injuries.
