The General-Purpose Bed (RB-GP400) is a medical surge bed for the general population and support staff. This lightweight, portable cot is great for the rigors of long or short term deployment. It has spun tube ends and an anti-microbial, waterproof mattress, with a comfortable spring and deck design. With a 450 pound working capacity, the sleeping surface is 32″ x 80″ at a height of 22″. This bed meets FED STD 1633 for flammability and is ADA compliant.
