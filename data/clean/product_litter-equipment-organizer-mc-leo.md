The Litter Equipment Organizer (MC-LEO) is a large equipment rack that protects litters, stands, pads and straps during storage and enables the mass movement/deployment of bulk supplies. Large casters enable this organizer to move easily on carpet, vinyl, wood and concrete flooring. The welded steel construction has no sharp edges and is ideal for the protection of medical assets. It features ten hinged doors, two steering handles, four locking swivel casters, and a see-through design. It can hold over 17 litters, 34 stands, 17 pads, and 34 straps.
