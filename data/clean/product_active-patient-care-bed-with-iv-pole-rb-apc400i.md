The Active Patient Care Bed w/ IV Pole (RB-APC400I) is a medical surge bed for continuous patient care. This taller, procedure height bed allows healthcare professionals to perform necessary functions at a working height. In the event of an overflow, it doubles effectively as a temporary cot. It is lightweight and portable, has a five position head elevator, two position foot elevator, anti-microbial mattress, spring and deck design, patient restraint belt, and a stow on board IV pole. With a 450 pound working capacity, the sleeping surface is 32″ x 80″ at a height of 28″. This bed meets FED STD 1633 for flammability.
