# Beds

Beds are an integral part of emergency preparedness and response operations. VeriCor beds are great solutions for the EP community. Built to federal emergency management’s specifications, these beds are used for deployment logistics, portable shelters, fixed hospitals, homeless sheltering programs, shelter-in-place services, and mass care evacuation.
