**WHO** IS A SYSTEM TRAINING SERVICE FOR?  
Previous customers that have any emergency preparedness system built by VeriCor. This includes public health departments, hospitals, VAMCs, or emergency management teams that have purchased products from VeriCor in the past.
**WHAT** IS A SYSTEM TRAINING SERVICE?  
A service where we come to you to teach you and your staff on how to effectively manage, deploy, and recover your system. Various levels of customer involvement available.
**WHEN** IS A SYSTEM TRAINING SERVICE USED?  
When time, personnel, or resources are scarce, yet EP staff needs to be up-to-date and ready to respond.
**WHERE** CAN A SYSTEM TRAINING SERVICE BE USED?  
A training can happen anywhere…from in an office to an on-site deployment location.
**WHY** BUY A SYSTEM TRAINING SERVICE?  
It’s easier than the alternative.
