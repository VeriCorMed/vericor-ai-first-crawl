**WHO** IS A PAN-FLU CACHE SYSTEM FOR?  
Any health care facility or coalition needing an emergency preparedness cache of supplies specifically for an influenza pandemic.
**WHAT** IS A PAN-FLU CACHE SYSTEM?  
A system of “modules” containing supplies needed to treat patients during a pandemic.
Examination – items specific to monitoring vital signs and carrying out physical exams.  
Medical Equipment – items specific to creating a medical treatment environment.  
Patient Care – items needed to provide appropriate patient care.  
Suction O2 Adjuncts – items specific to conducting oral, endotracheal and gastrointestinal suctioning.  
Staff Personal Protective Equipment – items used to reduce staff exposure to hazards within a medical environment.
Each module contains supplies specific to that function.
Supplies are organized within stackable, bulk storage containers so that the system can be stored within a minimal footprint.
_The SmartBook™ brings it all together._  
A printed resource containing the system details including pictures and line item lists of quantities, locations, lot #s, expiration dates, etc. A CD containing digital files also included.
**WHEN** IS A PAN-FLU CACHE SYSTEM USED?  
During an influenza pandemic to rapidly administer vaccine to a surge of patients.
**WHERE** CAN A PAN-FLU CACHE SYSTEM BE USED?  
Any building of opportunity, outdoor space, or temporary shelter (tents).
