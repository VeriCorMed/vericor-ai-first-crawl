The Infection Control and Prevention PPE (25 Kits) Specialized Response Module provides basic, ready to use PPE kits for healthcare and support staff who will be in contact with a suspected, or confirmed, infectious disease patient. This Response-Friendly module contains individually packaged kits of PPE (according to size) that is intuitively organized and clearly labeled. It includes 1 Wheeled Medical Case, extended cuff nitrile gloves, impervious coveralls, N95 masks, face shields, hoods (head and neck covers), boot covers, aprons, and more. CDC compliant for Ebola as of 3/1/2020.
