The Medical Equipment & Supplies for DUV (SS-DUV-MES) is a module of equipment and supplies for the evacuation of ambulatory, wheel-chair and litter patients during emergencies or disasters. This basic life support (BLS) level of care module is intuitively organized, compactly stored, rapidly deployed, and easily maintained. The included SmartBook™ contains photos and critical data of all equipment and supplies (digital and print).
