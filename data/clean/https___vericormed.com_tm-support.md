### **_Data Logging_**
![Data-Logging Icon](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/Data-Logging-Icon.png)
  * **Programmable Logging Interval**
  * **USB Download (no software)**
  * **Alarm History Reporting**

  * Programmable logging interval from 1 minute to 24 hour observations.
  * USB Download (use any USB flash drive/memory stick).
  * Smart-Alarm™ (signals until acknowledged).
  * Triple display simultaneously shows minimum, maximum and current temperatures (°F/°C switchable).

**Temperature Range** : -50° to 70°C (-58° to 158°F)
**Resolution** : .01°
**Accuracy** : ± .25°
**Dimensions** : 4.25” x 2.75” x .75” @ 4 oz
**Probes/Bottles** : 2.5” x 1” bottle w/ sealed probe; 10’ cord
**Certificate** : multi-point calibration; individually-numbered
