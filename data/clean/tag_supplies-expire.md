A Question from Your Colleagues Cool Cube™ FAQ Question: Is buffering material needed?Answer: No. The phase change material (PCM) panels that control the temperature of the Cool Cube™ are very close to, if not at, the temperature of the product. So when packed-out, PCM surrounds the product (all 6 sides), protecting and maintaining its temperature.
