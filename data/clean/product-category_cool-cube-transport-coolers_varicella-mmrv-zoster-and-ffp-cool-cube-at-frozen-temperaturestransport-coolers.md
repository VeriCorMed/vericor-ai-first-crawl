# Varicella, MMRV, Zoster & FFP - Cool Cube™ at Frozen Temperatures Transport Coolers
Cool Cubes™ at Frozen Temperatures transport coolers are passively cooled, compact units that will maintain a consistent temperature range between -50°C and -15°C for 100+ hours. These units are ideal for frozen vaccine such as Varicella, MMRV and Zoster, as well as Fresh Frozen Plasma (FFP). Six removable panels eliminate the need for ice or electricity and make conditioning (and pack-out) quicker and easier than anything on the market. Cool Cubes™ for Frozen Temperatures transport coolers are the best compact thermal protection products available to maintain the frozen cold chain!
