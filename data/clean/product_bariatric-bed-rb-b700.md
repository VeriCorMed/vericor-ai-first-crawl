The Bariatric Bed (RB-B700) is a lightweight, portable, reusable cot for larger patients or multiple users. This extra sturdy bed has heavy duty locking legs with a 700-pound working capacity. The longer, taller design offers the bariatric population (or multiple users) a safe and comfortable bed in times of need–ideal for maximizing the limited space of a patient surge situation. It has spun tube ends, an anti-microbial mattress, is waterproof, and has a comfortable spring and deck design. The sleeping surface is 38″ x 84″ at a height of 22″. The bed meets FED STD 1633 for flammability.
