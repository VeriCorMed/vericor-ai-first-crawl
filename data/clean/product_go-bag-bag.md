The Bag Go-Bag (MC-GB) is a wheeled duffle bag that gives medical supplies the organization needed for a quick response. This is the same bag used to hold the Vaccinator 500 Go-Bag vaccination system. In-line skate wheels, a retractable locking pull handle, padded carry handles, and an adjustable buckle strap compliment the spacious zippered main compartment, front and side zipper pockets, and the hard-sided separate bottom section.
