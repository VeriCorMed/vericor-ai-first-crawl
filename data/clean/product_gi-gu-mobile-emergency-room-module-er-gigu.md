The GI/GU ER Module provides the necessary components for adult and pediatric naso-gastric/urethral catheter insertion and ostomy care. This ER (Emergency Room) module is utilized on-scene where advanced levels of care are required. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly. It includes a Medical Organizer w/ 4 Drawers & Table, 1 Resupply Case, Suction Pumps, Catheter Kits, Hypoallergenic Tape, Colostomy Bags, Syringes, Ostomy Paste, and more.
