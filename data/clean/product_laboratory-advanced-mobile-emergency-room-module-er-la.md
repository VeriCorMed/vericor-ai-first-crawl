The Laboratory Advanced Mobile Emergency Room Module (ER-LA) provides the necessary components to preform bedside analysis for chemistries, electrolytes, lactate, coagulations, hematology, blood gases and cardiac markers. This ER (Emergency Room) module is utilized on-scene where advanced levels of care are required. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly™. It includes an i-STAT® Portable Handheld w/ Protective Case, Test Cartridges, Downloader, Printer, Simulator, and more.
