The Examination Pan-Flu Cache Module includes components specific to monitoring vital signs and carrying out physical exams. This Pan-Flu Cache module is designed to be utilized during an influenza pandemic and includeds support for children to adults. It is intuitively organized and ready to become part of a Response-Friendly, all-hazards, emergency stockpile. It includes 3 Response-Ready Containers, vital signs monitors in protective cases (w/ stands), blood pressure kits, scales (w/ height rods), oto/ophthalmoscope sets, thermometers, and more.
