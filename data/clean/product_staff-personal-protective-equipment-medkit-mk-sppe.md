The Staff Personal Protective Equipment medical kit (MK-SPPE) is a jump bag for protecting staff, while performing patient care, during emergency situations. Supplies include glasses, gloves, gowns, masks, sanitizer, etc. Prepackaged into an Upright Medical Organizer (MC-UMO), supplies are intuitively packaged and ready to use. Roll it open and hang it on the included easy to assemble stand (MC-SUMO). Store it in your choice of case: MC-1 (hard case) or MC-BUMO (soft case). A SmartBook™ containing photos and critical data also included.
