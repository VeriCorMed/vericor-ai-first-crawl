### TMDs (Data Loggers)![Simple and Easy to Use Data Loggers](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Simple-and-Easy-to-Use-Data-Loggers-300x200.jpg)
#### Simple and Easy to Use
Record, download, and analyze results with ease. The data logging TMDs (temperature monitoring devices) we carry:  
1. Are individually calibrated.  
2. Have a programmable logging interval.   
3. Have a buffered temperature probe(s).  
4. Have an alarm.  
5. Provides data as a simple spreadsheet (no software needed).  
6. Are easy to buy (direct or via Amazon).
