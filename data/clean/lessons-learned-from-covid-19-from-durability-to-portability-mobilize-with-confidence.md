### A) Pandemic Preparedness
#### Lessons Learned from COVID-19 and the Way Forward
In the wake of COVID-19, pandemic preparedness has become an essential topic. [This article ](https://www.aamc.org/media/56251/download)(found on the Association of American Medical Colleges website) provides an overview of the lessons learned from the COVID-19 pandemic and how we can use them to improve pandemic preparedness in the future. The authors discuss the importance of early detection and rapid response, the need for effective communication strategies, and the role of public health interventions in controlling the spread of pandemics.
