The Compact Response Case (MC-CRC) is a medium capacity case for medical supply organization. Latches keep contents secure during transport, while locks and a key keep items safe during storage. The removable tray has two covered compartments just right for smaller items. Pack it up and be ready to respond. It features an large compartment, wide, heavy-duty rubber-coated wheels, a large pull-out steel handle, and complimentary alphanumeric identification labeling.
