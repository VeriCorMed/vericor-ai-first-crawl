The Infection Control and Prevention PPE Kit, Large (SS-ICPK-LG) provides ready to use PPE for healthcare and support staff who will be in contact with a suspected, or confirmed, infectious disease patient. This single kit contains large-sized PPE gear in a small, compact package. It includes extended cuff nitrile gloves, an impervious coverall, N95 mask, face shield, hood (head and neck cover), boot covers, apron, and more. CDC compliant for Ebola as of 3/1/2020.
