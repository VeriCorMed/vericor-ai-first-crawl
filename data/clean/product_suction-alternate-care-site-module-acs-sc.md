The Suction ACS Module includes the components necessary to conduct oral, endotracheal and gastrointestinal suctioning. This ACS (Alternate Care Site) module is utilized during a patient surge in the wake of a disaster. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly. It includes a Medical Organizer w/ 4 Drawers & Table, 1 Additional Primary Medical Case, 1 Resupply Case, Suction Pumps, Catheter Kits, Canisters, Yankauers, Syringe Bulbs, Gloves, and more.
