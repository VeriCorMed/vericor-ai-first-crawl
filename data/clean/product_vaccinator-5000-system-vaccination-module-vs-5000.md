The Vaccinator 5000 System (VS-5000) is a set of supplies for the vaccination of 5000 people. This prepackage module is ideal for pandemic preparedness or a large flu clinic. Supplies are complete and organized so all that is needed are staff and vaccine. It includes a 42” Workstation w/ 6 Drawers & Table (MW-42), three resupply cases (MC-1), biohazard containers, sharps containers, a SmartBook™ (the critical data including descriptions, locations, lot numbers, expiration dates, storage parameters, etc.), and more (alcohol pads, bandages, syringes, gauze, gloves, sanitizer, spill kits, paper, pens, etc.). This system is ready to move into any place of opportunity in emergency situations. A line item list of products is available upon request.
