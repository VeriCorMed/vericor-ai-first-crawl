The Intravenous Therapy Adult Alternate Care Site provides the components necessary to administer fluid hydration and antibiotics to adults. This ACS (Alternate Care Site) module is utilized during a patient surge in the wake of a disaster. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly™. It includes 1 Upright Medical Organizer (UMO) within a Primary Medical Case, 1 Resupply Case, 1 Hand Truck, Pressure Infuser Bags, Intraosseous Needles, Start Kits, IV Extension Sets, Tubing, Fluids, and more.
