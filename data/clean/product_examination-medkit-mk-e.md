The Examination medical kit (MK-E) is a jump bag for monitoring vital signs and carrying out physical exams. Products include sphygmomanometer kits, stethoscopes, ophthalmoscope set, probes, thermometers, etc. Prepackaged into an Upright Medical Organizer (MC-UMO), supplies are intuitively packaged and ready to use. Roll it open and hang it on the included easy to assemble stand (MC-SUMO). Store it in your choice of case: MC-1 (hard case) or MC-BUMO (soft case). A SmartBook™ containing photos and critical data also included.
