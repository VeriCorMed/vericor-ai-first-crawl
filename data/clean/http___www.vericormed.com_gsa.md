**VeriCor, LLC GSA Federal Supply Schedule Contract GS-07F-082CA**
  
**Contract Information**  
VeriCor, LLC holds a GSA Multiple Award Schedule Contract. Eligible customers can purchase directly from VeriCor at special pre-negotiated prices on this Schedule.
**Products on GSA Contract**  
Most of the major products listed on this website are on one of our contracts. Parts and minor accessories are not, but may offered with courtesy open market discounts to eligible customers.
**Eligibility for GSA Contract Purchasing**  
All United States government organizations, military branches and civilian entities are eligible. Most Federal prime contractors are eligible also. Contractors not already eligible may be able to obtain temporary authorization to use GSA Schedule contracts. More on eligibility determinations can be found [here](https://www.gsa.gov/policy-regulations/policy/acquisition-policy/eligibility-determinations).
Contact your Contracting Officer or Procurement Department if you have questions about your eligibility.
**Obtaining a GSA Price Quote or Product Selection Assistance**
Option 1: Visit GSA Advantage and find/buy eligible products.  
Option 2: Send an email to info@vericormed.com noting the product and that you wish to have GSA pricing.  
Option 3: Call (608) 526-6901 with the product you desire pricing on. Indicate you wish to have GSA pricing.
To research VeriCor, LLC products on your own, browse this website, visit GSA Advantage or request information brochures from our office (info@vericormed.com or (608) 526-6901).
**Ordering and Payment Procedures**  
GSA orders can be placed directly with VeriCor using most common ordering methods, i.e., mail, fax, email, etc. If all quoted items are on our GSA contracts, you can even order online at [**GSA Advantage**](https://www.gsaadvantage.gov/advantage/ws/search/advantage_search?q=0:8vericor&db=0&searchType=0)!
