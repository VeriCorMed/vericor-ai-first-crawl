The Rolling Rack for 12 Bariatric Beds (RR-12B) is a rolling storage and deployment rack for 12 Bariatric Beds. This rack is constructed of heavy-duty steel with a baked-on, powder-coat finish to protect it from dings and scratches during exercises and/or deployment. Four wheels (two swivel and two fixed) ensure dynamic maneuverability and ease of movement on almost any surface. The rack features a welded-steel construction with laser-cut components for no sharp edges. Let these two shelves support the beds needed for those emergency preparation and mitigation plans.
