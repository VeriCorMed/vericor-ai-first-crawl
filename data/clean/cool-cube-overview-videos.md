# Cool Cube™ Overview Videos
There are four sizes of Cool Cube™ the 03, 08, 28 and 96. The Cool Cube™ is a qualified cooler and pack-out solution used to mobilize temperature-sensitive products without electricity, ice, or buffering material.
* * *
## Cool Cube™ 03
The 03 is very mobile and is ideal for small quantities. The capacity is approximately 3 liters, with a pack-out space of 11”x11”x11”. The unit weighs roughly 11 lbs.
## Cool Cube™ 08
The 08 is ideal for small to medium quantities. The capacity is approximately 8 liters, with a pack-out space of 11 ¼ x 6 ½ x 6 ½ “. The unit weighs between 16 to 20 lbs. depending on the PCM panels being used.
## Cool Cube™ 28
The 28 is ideal for large quantities, and is very mobile. It has outside dimensions of approximately 20″ x 20″ x 23″, with an internal packable space of 12″ x 12″ x 12″ which is approximately 28 liters.
## Cool Cube™ 96
The 96 is ideal for extra large quantities, and is very mobile. It has outside dimensions of approximately 26″ x 26″ x 28″, with an internal packable space of 18″ x 18″ x 18″ which is approximately 96 liters.
