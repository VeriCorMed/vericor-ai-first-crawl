### A) TMD and DDL Requirements
#### Be in compliance with the CDC.
From the 2019 Toolkit (p. 9):
  * Every vaccine storage and transport (emergency or non-emergency) unit must have a TMD.*
  * Use DDLs with a current and valid Certificate of Calibration Testing.
  * Keep the recorded data for three years or longer.
  * Have at least one backup TMD.

_*CDC recommends a specific type of TMD called a “digital data logger” (DDL)._
VeriCor took these requirements into account when designing Cool Cube™ PCM transport coolers. With integrated pockets and cord management features, these cold chain coolers are ideal for the safety conscious professional.
