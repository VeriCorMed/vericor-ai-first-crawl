The GI/GU Pediatric ACS Module includes the essential items necessary to conduct pediatric nasogastric/urethral catheter insertion. This ACS (Alternate Care Site) module is utilized during a patient surge in the wake of a disaster. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly. It includes a Medical Organizer w/ 4 Drawers & Table, Suction Pumps, Catheter Kits, Hypoallergenic Tape, Colostomy Bags, Syringes, and more.
