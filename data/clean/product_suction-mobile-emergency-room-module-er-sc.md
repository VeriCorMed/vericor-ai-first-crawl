The Suction ER Module provides the necessary components for conducting oral, endotracheal and gastrointestinal suctioning. It includes a Medical Organizer w/ 4 Drawers & Table, 1 Additional Primary Medical Case, 1 Resupply Case, Suction Pumps, Catheter Kits, Canisters, Syringe Bulbs, Gloves, and more.
