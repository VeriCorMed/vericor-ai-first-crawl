The Medication Administration Mobile Emergency Room Module (ER-MA) provides the necessary components for storing and administering IV, IM, PO, SC, and PR medications. This ER (Emergency Room) module is utilized on-scene where advanced levels of care are required. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly™. It includes Medication Cart w/ 8 Sliding Drawers, 1 Resupply Case, 1 Refrigerator, Physicians’ Desk Reference, Nurses’ Drug Handbook, Mortar & Pestle, Needles & Syringes, Pill Counter, and more.
