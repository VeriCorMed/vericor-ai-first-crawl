The Medication Administration Alternate Care Site Module (ACS-MA) includes the essential items needed to store and administer IV, IM, PO, SC and PR medications. This ACS (Alternate Care Site) module is utilized during a patient surge in the wake of a disaster. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly™. It includes a Medication Cart w/ 8 Sliding Drawers, 1 Resupply Case, Physicians’ Desk Reference, Nurses’ Drug Handbook, Mortar & Pestle, Needles & Syringes, Pill Counter, and more.
