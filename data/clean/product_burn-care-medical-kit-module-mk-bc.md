The Burn Care medical kit (MK-BC) is a jump bag for debridement and dressing of minor and major burns. Supplies include essential PPE, debridement kits, disposable cautery, silver sulfadiazine, sterile dressings & gauzes, etc. Prepackaged into an Upright Medical Organizer (MC-UMO), supplies are intuitively packaged and ready to use. Roll it open and hang it on the included easy to assemble stand (MC-SUMO). Store it in your choice of case: MC-1 (hard case) or MC-BUMO (soft case). A SmartBook™ containing photos and critical data also included.
