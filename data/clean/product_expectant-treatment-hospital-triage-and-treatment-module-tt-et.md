The Expectant Treatment Hospital Triage & Treatment Module provides the components necessary for medical staff to address victims who would not survive with optimal medical care. This piece of the complete Triage & Treatment System is utilized at the scene of an accident or mass-casualty incident. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly. It includes 2 Mobile Medical Cases, 1 Upright Medical Organizer, staff personal protective equipment, and post-mortem supplies.
