# Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 28

**Summary:** An outer case that snugly fits the Temp-Shield™ Insulation System to create the portable Cool Cube™ 28 PCM cooler. This robust case is a standard tan with wheels, a tow handle, external pocket, and lifting handles. *(VIP & PCM Panels not included.)*

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 28 (CC-Case-28) is a hard-sided, heavy-duty case customized to fit the Temp-Shield™ vacuum insulated panel (VIP) system of Cool Cube™ 28 models. The case features a clip-on towing strap for pulling the unit, fold-down side handles for easy, two-person lifting, double-throw latches for easy opening, and four swivel wheels (two locking) for ease of movement. This case is specifically designed to facilitate easy product transport.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
