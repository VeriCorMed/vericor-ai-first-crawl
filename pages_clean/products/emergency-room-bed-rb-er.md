# Emergency Room Bed

**Summary:** A portable, easy to use stainless steel emergency bed for medical surge capacity. This well constructed bed has an adjustable backrest for patient comfort or treatment needs. Push-button design deploys the bed without the use of tools.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Emergency Room Bed (RB-ER) is a stainless steel emergency room bed for safe, efficient field treatment. The heavy-duty construction features a hard deck for rigorous medical procedures, while an adjustable height and backrest provide for a variety of field medicine needs. Push-button assembly allows for a quick setup without tools, and after use, all parts can be easily disinfected and decontaminated. It includes a single-hook IV pole, removable side rails, anti-microbial mattress, and a set of four locking casters. The bed surface is 31″ x 81″ with a 500-pound working capacity (height is adjustable from 19″ to 32″). This bed is ADA compliant.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
