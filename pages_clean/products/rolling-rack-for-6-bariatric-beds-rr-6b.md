# Rolling Rack for 6 Bariatric Beds

**Summary:** A rolling storage and deployment rack for 6 Bariatric Beds. This heavy duty steel rack has two fixed and two swivel casters (3” diameter) for easy movement on most surfaces.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Rolling Rack for 6 Bariatric Beds (RR-6B) is a rolling storage and deployment rack for 6 Bariatric Beds. This rack is constructed of heavy-duty steel with a baked-on, powder-coat finish to protect it from dings and scratches during exercises and/or deployment. Four wheels (two swivel and two fixed) ensure dynamic maneuverability and ease of movement on almost any surface. The rack features a welded-steel construction with laser-cut components for no sharp edges.
