# Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 08

**Summary:** An outer case that snugly fits the Temp-Shield™ Insulation System to create the portable Cool Cube™ 08 PCM cooler. This fabric bag is a standard blue with a carrying handle, shoulder strap, clear pockets and double slide zipper. *(VIP & PCM Panels not included.)*

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text css=""]The Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 08 (CC-Case-08) is a soft-sided bag made up of a lightweight, durable material, that fits the Temp-Shield™ vacuum insulated panel (VIP) system of Cool Cube™ 08 models. The case features an adjustable, padded shoulder strap with a built-in carrying handle, clear outer pockets and a double-zipper design. This case is specifically made to make transport easy.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
