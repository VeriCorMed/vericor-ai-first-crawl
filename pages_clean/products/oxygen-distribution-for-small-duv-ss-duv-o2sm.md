# Oxygen Distribution (for Small DUV)

**Summary:** An oxygen distribution kit used to deliver bed-side oxygen to patients within a small Dual Use Vehicle (DUV). Module has a compact footprint and can rapidly be deployed. (Made specifically for the Dept. of Veterans Affairs.)

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Oxygen Distribution for Small DUV (SS-DUV-O2SM) is a kit for delivering bed-side oxygen to patients within a small Dual Use Vehicle (DUV). This kit, specifically made for the Dept. of Veterans Affairs, has a compact footprint and can rapidly be deployed. All components are contained within a wheeled, labeled, protective case for safe storage and easy movement. The cascade system and manifold attach to the L-track within the DUV for rapid set-up and safe oxygen distribution. It has three oxygen cascade systems (one per litter station), each with three patient drops (one per litter), along with one oxygen manifold accommodating up to eight patients. A SmartBook™ containing photos and critical data also included.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
