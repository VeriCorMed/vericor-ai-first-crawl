The Administrative Support Alternate Care Site Module (ACS-AS) Module provides the components necessary for medical staff to coordinate and supervise daily field operations. This ACS (Alternate Care Site) module is utilized during a patient surge in the wake of a disaster. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly™. It includes a 4-drawer Medical Organizer w/ Table, 3 Additional Primary Medical Cases, Command Board w/ EMIC Pack, Easels, All-in-One Printer, Megaphone, 2-Way Radios, Clipboards, Markers, and more.
