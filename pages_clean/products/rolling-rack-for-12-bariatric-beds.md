# Rolling Rack for 12 Bariatric Beds

**Summary:** A rolling storage and deployment rack for 12 Bariatric Beds. This heavy duty steel rack has 2 shelves along with fixed and swivel casters for easy movement on most surfaces.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Rolling Rack for 12 Bariatric Beds (RR-12B) is a rolling storage and deployment rack for 12 Bariatric Beds. This rack is constructed of heavy-duty steel with a baked-on, powder-coat finish to protect it from dings and scratches during exercises and/or deployment. Four wheels (two swivel and two fixed) ensure dynamic maneuverability and ease of movement on almost any surface. The rack features a welded-steel construction with laser-cut components for no sharp edges. Let these two shelves support the beds needed for those emergency preparation and mitigation plans.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
