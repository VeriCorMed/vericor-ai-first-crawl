# Medical Equipment & Supplies (for DUV)

**Summary:** A medical kit used to supplement Dual Use Vehicles (DUVs) with equipment and supplies necessary to evacuate ambulatory, wheel-chair and litter patients during emergencies or disasters. (Made specifically for the Dept. of Veterans Affairs.)

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Medical Equipment & Supplies for DUV (SS-DUV-MES) is a module of equipment and supplies for the evacuation of ambulatory, wheel-chair and litter patients during emergencies or disasters. This basic life support (BLS) level of care module is intuitively organized, compactly stored, rapidly deployed, and easily maintained. The included SmartBook™ contains photos and critical data of all equipment and supplies (digital and print).[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
