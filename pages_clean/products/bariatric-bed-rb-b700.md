# Bariatric Bed (700# wt cap)

**Summary:** A wider, taller, and longer surge bed for larger (or multiple) patients. This versatile 700+ pound weight capacity bed offers comfort and safety to patients when they need it most. It is lightweight, portable, and reusable.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Bariatric Bed (RB-B700) is a lightweight, portable, reusable cot for larger patients or multiple users. This extra sturdy bed has heavy duty locking legs with a 700-pound working capacity. The longer, taller design offers the bariatric population (or multiple users) a safe and comfortable bed in times of need--ideal for maximizing the limited space of a patient surge situation. It has spun tube ends, an anti-microbial mattress, is waterproof, and has a comfortable spring and deck design. The sleeping surface is 38″ x 84″ at a height of 22″. The bed meets FED STD 1633 for flammability.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
