The Delayed Treatment with Closed System IV Catheters Hospital Triage & Treatment Module provides the components necessary for medical staff to care for casualties who require hospitalization and prolonged surgery. This piece of the complete Triage & Treatment System is utilized at the scene of an accident or mass-casualty incident. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly. It includes 1 Rolling Chest, 1 Medical Organizer w/ 4 Drawers & Table, 5 Upright Medical Organizers,and supplies/equipment to address the following care functions: staff personal protective equipment, examination, intravenous therapy (utilizing closed system IV catheters), orthopedics, wound care, and wound closure (with a needle decomp kit).

#### **Delayed Treatment with Closed System IV Catheters Hospital Triage and Treatment Module Equipment Includes:**

* 1 - Rolling Chest
* 1 - Medical Organizer w/ 4 Drawers & Table
* 4 - Upright Medical Organizers (UMO)
