# Upright Medical Organizer

**Summary:** An exclusive roll pack organizer for emergency preparedness supplies. Made of durable vinyl, this pack provides to visibility to supplies organized within the zippered pockets. Multiple hanging features allows vertical deployment almost anywhere.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Upright Medical Organizer (MC-UMO) is an exclusive, hangable roll pack for organizing medical supplies needing visibility. This jump-bag style organizer is made of durable, decontaminable vinyl that can be hung on doors, shelter frames or stands. Pack the 20 clear pockets with supplies, zipper the pockets close, and roll it up to carry it into the field. Pre-attached straps and hooks enable various hanging capabilities, while the highly visible ID holder tells providers what it is for.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
