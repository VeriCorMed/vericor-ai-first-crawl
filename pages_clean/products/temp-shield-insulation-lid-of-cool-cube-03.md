# Temp-Shield™ Insulation Lid of Cool Cube™ 03

**Summary:** A vacuum insulation panel that acts as the lid of the Cool Cube™ 03. This panel provides advanced thermal protection for temperature sensitive product by utilizing vacuum insulation technology for the lid of the cooler.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Temp-Shield™ Insulation Lid of Cool Cube™ 03 (CC-VIPLID-03) is a replacement lid for the original insulation system of the Cool Cube™ 03.
