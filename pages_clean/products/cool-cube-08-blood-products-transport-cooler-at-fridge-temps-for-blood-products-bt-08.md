# Cool Cube™ 08 at Fridge Temps

**Summary:** A laboratory validated cooler that utilizes PCM technology to keep product such as blood products, tissues, and organs 1-6°C for 53+ hours without the need for electricity, ice or buffering material. Ideal for extended RCB storage (or transportation), field-ops and power outage backup.

[vc\_row][vc\_column width="1/2"][vc\_column\_text]

The Cool Cube™ 08 at Fridge Temperatures (BT-08) is not an ordinary blood products transport cooler. It is an easy-to-carry, qualified container and pack-out system that keeps temperature sensitive product such as blood products, tissues, and organs chilled between 1° and 10°C for over 3 days, or between 1° and 6°C for over 2 days– without using ice or electricity! This cooler is ideal for transport, but also excels at protecting valuable product during off-site use and power outages, regardless of hot or freezing conditions. The simplified, validated, patented technology is the best compact thermal protection product available to maintain the cold chain!

Cool Cube™ PCM (phase change material) is engineered to maintain a constant temperature at a specific melt/freeze point. PCM empowers the user with a safe, effective means to maintain the temperature of valuable product.

[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][vc\_column width="1/3" css=".vc\_custom\_1545864566465{background-color: #f9f9f9 !important;}"][vc\_column\_text]**![](https://www.vericormed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/green-clipart-circle-2-150x150.png)Temp-Shield™ Insulation**

Vacuum insulated panels (VIPs) offer advanced thermal protection far superior to other types of insulation. The temperature-holding qualities ensures the user a long-lasting, consistent temperature hold for days.

Each outer case is built with mobility in mind. Various features enable the user to move with confidence and know the contents are well protected (and at the correct temperature) during transit and use.
