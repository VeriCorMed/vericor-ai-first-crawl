# 42” Workstation w/ 6 Drawers & Table

**Summary:** A mobile workstation with six drawers for storage, plus a detachable table for working on. Undo a couple latches & it opens up, ready for work. This self-contained case is ideal for in-field clinics needing to store & transport medical supplies.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The 42” Workstation w/ 6 Drawers & Table (MC-42) is a mobile workstation for medical supply storage and mobility. This self-contained case on wheels has ample storage, transport handles and a detachable table that can move when and where it is needed. It is lockable for secure storage, has glove holders in the lid for easy access, and easy lock wheels with a wide wheel base for easy/safe maneuverability.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
