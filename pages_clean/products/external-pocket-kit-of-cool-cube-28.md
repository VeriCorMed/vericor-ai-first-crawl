# External Pocket Kit of Cool Cube™ 28

**Summary:** An external pocket kit for the older Cool Cube™ 28 models that didn’t have the current outer pocket and cord port (mounting hardware included).

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]

The External Pocket Kit of Cool Cube™ 28 (CC-EPRK) is a retrofit kit for any tan, hard-cased Cool Cube™ 28 purchased prior to 2016. This kit adds an external pocket and flexible grommet to the outside of the case. These two features (now standard on the Cool Cube™ 28) enable the user to keep a monitor on the outside of the case during transport. Some assembly is required.

**TOOLS NEEDED (not included)**

- Channellock Pliers (or similar)
- Drill
- Hammer
- Phillips-Head Screwdriver
- Small Wrench (Crescent or 5/16”)
