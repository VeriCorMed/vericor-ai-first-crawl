# Mobile Charging Station

**Summary:** A unique equipment chest with built-in electrical and shelving for charging EP supplies. Doors swing open to reveal built-in door shelving and main compartment wire shelving. Keep equipment organized and fully charged with this exclusive case.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Mobile Charging Station (MC-MCS) is a unique equipment chest for keeping medical equipment organized and fully charged. With built-in electrical, this durable case protects assets during storage and deployment, while providing a lockable solution that organizes charging stations and power supplies. Pack it up and be ready to respond. It features carriage doors with shelves, seven swivel casters (2 locking), an overhead light, premium power strips, wire shelving, and bottom shelf tie downs.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
