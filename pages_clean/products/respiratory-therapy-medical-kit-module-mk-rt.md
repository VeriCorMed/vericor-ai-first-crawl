# Respiratory Therapy

**Summary:** A medical kit loaded with essential components needed for the administration of oxygen and inhalation therapy. This emergency preparedness MedKit has medical supplies organized into the pockets of a jump bag for those disaster response scenarios.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Respiratory Therapy medical kit (MK-RT) is a jump bag for the administration of oxygen and inhalation therapy. Supplies include peak flow meters, saline, masks, tubing, etc. Prepackaged into an Upright Medical Organizer (MC-UMO), supplies are intuitively packaged and ready to use. Roll it open and hang it on the included easy to assemble stand (MC-SUMO). Store it in your choice of case: MC-1 (hard case) or MC-BUMO (soft case). A SmartBook™ containing photos and critical data also included.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
