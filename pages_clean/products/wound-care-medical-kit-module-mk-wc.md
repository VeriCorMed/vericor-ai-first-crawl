# Wound Care

**Summary:** A medical kit loaded with components needed for treating & managing minor burns, lacerations, punctures, abrasions, and abscesses. This emergency preparedness MedKit has supplies organized into the pockets of a jump bag for those disaster scenarios.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Wound Care medical kit (MK-WC) is a jump bag for treating and managing minor burns, lacerations, punctures, abrasions, and abscesses. Supplies include gloves, tape, dressing, bandages, etc. Prepackaged into an Upright Medical Organizer (MC-UMO), supplies are intuitively packaged and ready to use. Roll it open and hang it on the included easy to assemble stand (MC-SUMO). Store it in your choice of case: MC-1 (hard case) or MC-BUMO (soft case). A SmartBook™ containing photos and critical data also included.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
