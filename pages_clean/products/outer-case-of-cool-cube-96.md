# Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 96

**Summary:** An outer case that snugly fits the Temp-Shield™ Insulation System to create the portable Cool Cube™ 96 PCM cooler. This robust case is a standard tan with wheels, a tow strap, external pocket, and lifting handles. *(VIP & PCM Panels not included.)*

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Outer Case of Cool Cube™ 96 (CC-Case-96) is a hard-sided, heavy-duty case customized to fit the Temp-Shield™ vacuum insulated panel (VIP) system of Cool Cube™ 96 models. The case features a folding handle on top for pulling the unit, fold-down side handles for easy, two-person lifting, double-throw latches for easy opening, and a Tru-Trac System™ of wheels for ease of movement. This case is specifically designed to facilitate easy product transport.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
