# Active Patient Care Bed w/ IV Pole

**Summary:** A medical surge bed for continuous patient care. This 28” tall (procedure height) bed presents patients at a comfortable height for healthcare professionals. The stow-on-board IV pole folds up with the rest of the bed.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Active Patient Care Bed w/ IV Pole (RB-APC400I) is a medical surge bed for continuous patient care. This taller, procedure height bed allows healthcare professionals to perform necessary functions at a working height. In the event of an overflow, it doubles effectively as a temporary cot. It is lightweight and portable, has a five position head elevator, two position foot elevator, anti-microbial mattress, spring and deck design, patient restraint belt, and a stow on board IV pole. With a 450 pound working capacity, the sleeping surface is 32″ x 80″ at a height of 28″. This bed meets FED STD 1633 for flammability.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
