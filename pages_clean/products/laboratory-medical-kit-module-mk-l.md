# Laboratory

**Summary:** A medical kit loaded with components needed for performing phlebotomy and point-of-care testing (POCT). This emergency preparedness MedKit has medical supplies organized into the pockets of a jump bag for those disaster response scenarios.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Laboratory medical kit (MK-L) is a jump bag for performing phlebotomy and point-of-care testing (POCT), including Strep A, pregnancy and diabetes. Supplies include glucometer, test strips, specimen cups, etc. Prepackaged into an Upright Medical Organizer (MC-UMO), supplies are intuitively packaged and ready to use. Roll it open and hang it on the included easy to assemble stand (MC-SUMO). Store it in your choice of case: MC-1 (hard case) or MC-BUMO (soft case). A SmartBook™ containing photos and critical data also included.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
