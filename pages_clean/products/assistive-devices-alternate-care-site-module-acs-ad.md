The Assistive Devices Alternate Care Site Module (ACS-AS) Module includes the components necessary to assist patients with injuries and/or physical disabilities. This ACS (Alternate Care Site) module is utilized during a patient surge in the wake of a disaster. It is intuitively organized and packaged to be Response-Friendly™. It includes 1 Primary Medical Case, Bi-Fold Litters, Wheelchairs, Commodes, Crutches, Walkers, Communication Picture Board, and more.
