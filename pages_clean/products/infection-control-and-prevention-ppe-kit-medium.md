# ICP PPE Kit (Medium)

**Summary:** A prepackaged kit of personal protective equipment (PPE), size medium. This equipment is worn to minimize exposure to a variety of hazards. Ideal for healthcare and support staff in contact with suspected, or confirmed, infectious disease patients.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Infection Control and Prevention PPE Kit, Medium (SS-ICPK-MD) provides ready to use PPE for healthcare and support staff who will be in contact with a suspected, or confirmed, infectious disease patient. This single kit contains medium-sized PPE gear in a small, compact package. It includes extended cuff nitrile gloves, an impervious coverall, N95 mask, face shield, hood (head and neck cover), boot covers, apron, and more.
