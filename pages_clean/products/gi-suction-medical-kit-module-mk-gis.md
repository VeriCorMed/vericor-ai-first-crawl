# GI/Suction

**Summary:** A medical kit with components to conduct catheter insertions and oral, endotracheal and gastrointestinal suctioning. This preparedness MedKit has supplies organized into the pockets of a jump bag and two protective cases for disaster scenarios.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The GI/Suction medical kit (MK-GIS) is a jump bag and 2 protective cases of for conducting catheter insertions and oral, endotracheal and gastrointestinal suctioning. Supplies include suction pumps, catheter kits, canisters, yankauers, hypoallergenic tape, colostomy bags, etc. Prepackaged into an Upright Medical Organizer (MC-UMO), supplies are intuitively packaged and ready to use. Roll it open and hang it on the included easy to assemble stand (MC-SUMO). Store it in your choice of case: MC-1 (hard case) or MC-BUMO (soft case). The protective cases are wheeled, have a retractable extension handle, and have foam protecting the valuable equipment. A SmartBook™ containing photos and critical data also included.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
