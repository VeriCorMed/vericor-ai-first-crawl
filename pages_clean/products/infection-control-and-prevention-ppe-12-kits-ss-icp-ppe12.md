# Infection Control and Prevention PPE (12 Kits)

**Summary:** A prepackaged module of 12 personal protective equipment (PPE) kits. This equipment is worn to minimize exposure to a variety of hazards. Ideal for healthcare and support staff in contact with suspected, or confirmed, infectious disease patients.

[vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]The Infection Control and Prevention PPE (12 Kits) Specialized Response Module provides basic, ready to use PPE kits for healthcare and support staff who will be in contact with a suspected, or confirmed, infectious disease patient. This Response-Friendly module contains individually packaged kits of PPE (according to size) that is intuitively organized and clearly labeled. It includes 1 tote, extended cuff nitrile gloves, impervious coveralls, N95 masks, face shields, hoods (head and neck covers), boot covers, aprons, and more. CDC compliant for Ebola as of 3/1/2020.[/vc\_column\_text][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_row full\_width="stretch\_row\_content\_no\_spaces"][vc\_column][vc\_separator border\_width="2"][/vc\_column][/vc\_row][vc\_section][vc\_row][vc\_column][vc\_column\_text]
