### A) Protecting Vaccines During Transport
#### The Basics
To protect vaccines against a temperature excursion during transport, be sure to take the necessary precautions:  
_Use temperature-controlled packaging._  
Transporters should use packaging designed to maintain the temperature of vaccines during transportation. Be sure to adhere to the specific needs of the vaccine.
